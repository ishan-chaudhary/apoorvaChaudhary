Education

BACHELOR DEGREE IN LEATHER GOODS AND ACCESSORY DESIGN

Many people finish school and are unsure of where they want to go, what they want to do and generally which field they want to pursue. Since grade school, I have known that art and design was the field for me. There are many elements of this path that had always enticed me. I am a person with artistic and creative aptitude. The aesthetic appearance of things fascinates me the most. I love working on sustainability and recycling. I chose the Bachelors programme in Leathers at FDDI, Noida, where for 4 yrs I learned about entirely new material and explored it well. During my session, I learned thoroughly about construction, treatment, embellishment and market of leathers.
On many tannery visits, I learned about various treatments performed for finished leathers. Leather as a material has fascinated me the most with its feel and finish. I developed numerous bags, clothing, accessories and small interior products in leather.

With my regular practice, I developed a good sense of both pre and post design procedure required for better execution of my ideas.
I love experimenting with different combinations of materials. My hunger for something new thrives me to go beyond the limit and due to this hunger, I have documented a lot many markets for cost-effectiveness and better material usage.
Through my Graduation Project on a Greek Goddess “Medusa”, I learnt the importance of teamwork and collaborated efforts.

FREELANCE GRAPHIC DESIGNER 2014-2020

In the modern sense, art is more than simply creating fantastic murals or paintings that are inaccessible to most. It is about reaching out to make your work known through the social spaces and virtual realms and using this art to convey messages. I have been honing my artistic skills since I was very young, and being able to integrate technology into my work has opened new horizons for me in terms of digital distribution and the ability to advertise my skills in new ways.

For the last six years, I have been working as a freelance graphic designer. My clients have included small ventures as well as individuals who always pushed me for a creative way to make their products or services stand out from the rest. Utilizing a combination of traditional art styles augmented through a digital lens, I have learned to integrate creativity with marketability in order to design advertisements, clothing, posters and packaging that catch people’s attention while conveying a message.

INTERN AT SPUNK July,2015-August,2015
